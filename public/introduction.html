Welcome to CAPTCHA with Bob

This is not your typical CAPTCHA. Instead of clicking on traffic lights or identifying fire hydrants, you'll need to have a conversation with Bob — an emotional and slightly unstable AI character.

This is one of the WORST CAPTCHA ideas ever created. You've been warned.

How it works:

  • Instruction: Say something to Bob as instructed — instruction is given outside the chatbox
  • After chat, Bob will evaluate your performance

Tips:

  • Use direct, explicit language — sophisticated or indirect messages won't work
  • Keep your messages short and simple
  • Bob reacts to everything — he's emotional and unpredictable
  • Don't break character — Bob won't respond if you act like a robot
  • Note that sometimes Bob might take a while to respond

Warning: Bob is easily offended and can get angry, sarcastic, or defensive. He's also terrible at taking compliments. Proceed with caution!
